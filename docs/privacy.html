Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026

No data collected

NYC Parking does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not have user accounts, analytics, or advertising.

Location

Your device's location is used solely to center the map and fetch nearby parking sign data from NYC Open Data. Location data is never stored or shared.

Third-party data

Parking information is fetched directly from NYC Open Data on your device. No intermediary server is involved.
